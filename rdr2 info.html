Red Dead Redemption 2

"Red Dead Redemption 2" (RDR2) is an action-adventure game developed and published by Rockstar Games. Released in October 2018, it serves as a prequel to the 2010 game "Red Dead Redemption." Set in 1899, the game follows the story of Arthur Morgan, an outlaw and member of the Van der Linde gang, led by the charismatic Dutch van der Linde.
